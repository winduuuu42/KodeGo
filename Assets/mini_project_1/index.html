About

Hi my name is Brendan Tolentino, but you can call me Bren for short. I'm a photographer and collector of scale models. I started having these two as my hobby when I was in 14 years old. These were actually an influence from my parents whom guided me thoroughly. As I grow up this became not only my hobby but a part of me and my system. I really enjoy taking photos not only of my scale models but as well as other people, sceneries, cars, landscapes and many more. My goal is that one day I can share my experiences, photos and scale models that I took and build not only to my friends and family but to everyone.
